Warm-up:
Pike Push-ups – 3 sets × 10 reps (Regular Push-ups for beginners)
Shoulder Rotation – 2-3 minutes

Main Workout:

Lateral Raises (For Side Delts) – 4 sets × 12-15 reps

Dumbbell Overhead Press / Machine Overhead Press – 4 sets × 10 reps

Barbell Overhead Press – 4 sets × 8-10 reps

Reverse Butterfly (For Rear Delts) – 3 sets × 12 reps

Shrugs – 4 sets × 12-15 reps
