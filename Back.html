Back Workout

Warm-up:Dead Hang – 30-60 sec
Pull-ups – 3 sets × 5-10 reps (Scapula Pull-ups for beginners)

Main Workout:

Lat Pulldown (Wide Grip) – 4 sets × 10 reps

Lat Pulldown Behind Neck – 3 sets × 10 reps *(chin up grip can be done instead aswell)*

Machine Rows – 4 sets × 10 reps(barbel rows if machine not availible)

Dumbbell Rows – 4 sets × 10 reps

Cable Rows – 4 sets × 10 reps

Lower Back:

Hyperextensions – 3 sets × 15 reps (optional recommended if lower back pain)
